About Us

Maintain Your Property or Passion

  • Local Tri-State Area Expertise

    Nestled in South Point, Ohio, we're a fully insured team ideally situated to serve Southern Ohio, Eastern Kentucky, and Western West Virginia. Led by the father-and-son duo, Tim and Tyler Porter, we bring a wealth of knowledge about the unique maintenance needs of the Tri-State area.

  • Diverse Skill Set for Every Need

    Offering a wide range of services, we specialize in both residential and commercial cleaning solutions—from roof and house washing to gutter brightening and driveway maintenance. We also cater to your basic handyman requirements, utilizing top-tier equipment to ensure your job is done professionally and efficiently.

  • Family-Driven Commitment to Quality

    As a family-owned business founded in 2022, we infuse each project with a personal touch and unwavering commitment. Our clients are more than just customers; they're our neighbors and community. This deep-rooted connection to our work ensures a level of care and quality that truly sets us apart. Our guiding principle, inspired by scripture, resonates through our mission: "And whatsoever you do, do it heartily, as to the Lord, and not unto men."
